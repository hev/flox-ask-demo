Explains why Flox exists: teams repeatedly face inconsistent environments, dependency drift, and brittle build processes that don't scale across machines, teams, or deployment targets, and current solutions add complexity and fragmentation. Flox offers a consistent, language-agnostic workflow from local development to CI to production, addressing three common use cases: reproducible dev environments, reliable cross-system package management, and consistent builds from local to production.
