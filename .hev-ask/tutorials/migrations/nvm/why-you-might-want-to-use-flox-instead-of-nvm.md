Argues for Flox over nvm: it gives diverse teams a single activation entrypoint regardless of language, manages Node.js plus other dependencies and services together, supplies compatible build dependencies for native add-on compilation thanks to its Nixpkgs basis, and can also manage other JavaScript runtimes like Bun and Deno that nvm cannot.
