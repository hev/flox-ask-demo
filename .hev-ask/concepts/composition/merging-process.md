Details how composed manifests are merged before locking, with later manifests overriding earlier ones and the composing environment having highest priority. Describes per-section merge behavior: install, services, and vars take the union with conflicting entries overridden; hook and profile scripts are appended; containerize options are partly merged and partly overridden; and options are fully overridden, with a caution that a single included environment can surprisingly flip the activation mode.
