Describes the branches Flox maintains in its nixpkgs fork, which map to a not-yet-fully-exposed concept of stability channels reset on different cadences (daily, weekly, monthly, twice yearly). Warns these branches are distinct from similarly named upstream branches and that this architecture allows future stability selection plus patching and backporting.
