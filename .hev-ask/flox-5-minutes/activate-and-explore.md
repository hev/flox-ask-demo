Walks through activating the sample environment to make its packages available, confirming it works by running the sample project without manually installing its language, and listing the installed packages. Emphasizes that getting started on a project takes just cloning and activating, that Flox is language-agnostic so a whole multi-language toolchain lives in one place, and that everyone who activates gets the same packages.
