Walks through sharing the environment by pushing it to FloxHub, a platform for sharing and discovering environments and packages. Covers signing up, authenticating via the CLI (which opens a browser device-confirmation flow), pushing the environment, and viewing its manifest, packages, variables, services, generations, and supported platforms in the web interface.
